The Product Brief

Mintrix AI represents a paradigm shift in neural architecture. Unlike monolithic models of the past, Mintrix utilizes a **Liquid Transformer** approach, allowing weights to adapt dynamically based on contextual priority.

This technological foundation ensures that our systems are not just reactive, but predictive. By embedding privacy at the kernel level, we eliminate the traditional trade-off between performance and data safety.
