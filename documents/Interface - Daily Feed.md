## Purpose

The Daily Feed is the primary ambient surface for a persona's day. It is the "Pulse" of the system, designed to move the user from passive awareness to active engagement.

Unlike a traditional dashboard of static widgets, the Daily Feed is **time-aware**, **contextual**, and **proactive**.

---

## 1. Surface Characteristics

-   **Time-Anchored**: Content is ordered by relevance to the current moment (e.g., "Coming up in 10 minutes" vs. "Summary of yesterday").
-   **Mixed Content**: It blends schedule info, risk highlights, system recommendations, and recent autonomous actions.
-   **Low Density**: Focuses on "High Signal" items to prevent cognitive overload.
-   **Proactive**: It doesn't just show data; it suggests what should happen next.

---

## 2. Typical Content Blocks

| Block Type | Description | Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Schedule Pulse** | Real-time timetable and room changes. | *"Class 5B starts in 10 mins. Moved to Room 302."* |
| **Risk Highlights** | Early signals of academic or operational drift. | *"Syllabus drift detected in Grade 8 History."* |
| **Agent Nudges** | Proactive reminders for missing context or data. | *"3 students marked absent without parent notes."* |
| **Pending Actions** | High-level link to the Approval Inbox or Exception Center. | *"2 high-sensitivity parent drafts ready for review."* |
| **Autonomous Briefs** | Short summaries of what the AI did while you were away. | *"Sent 12 routine fee reminders this morning."* |

---

## 3. Persona-Specific Feed Behavior

-   **Teacher Feed**: Focuses on lesson readiness, student attendance, and class-level syllabus pace.
-   **Principal Feed**: Focuses on institutional coherence, staff availability, and major operational exceptions.
-   **Admin Feed**: Focuses on setup completion, event dependencies, and routine coordination tasks.

---

## 4. Interaction Patterns

1.  **The "Morning Briefing" Card**: A synthesized summary card that appears at the start of the day.
2.  **The "Swipe to Resolve"**: Low-friction way to acknowledge a nudge without entering a workspace.
3.  **The "Deep Dive" Route**: Every feed item acts as a gateway to either a **Workspace Surface** (for execution) or an **Exception Center** (for triage).

---

## 5. Design Rule: "Signal over Noise"

The Daily Feed fails if it becomes a "Notification Junk Drawer." Every item must have a clear reason for being there *now* and a clear path to resolution or awareness.
